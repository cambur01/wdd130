Overview

Purpose

The Purpose of this website is to build an online presence to bring more customers to our rafting business. Currently our only sources of customers are word of mouth and advertisements, but having a platform where we can attract online customers and provide more information to them will have a very positive impact on our revenue. We especially need to make a good impression with our home page, including what sets our business apart from other rafting businesses.

Audience

  • Q: Who are the target customers? | A: Adventurous groups and young families who love the outdoors. They are in a decent financial position and want to invest in fun experiences that they will remember for years to come.
  • Q: What do they want? | A: They want to make memories and have an exhilerating, yet stress-free experience.
  • Q: What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? | A: Information on the location, how long the experience will last, pricing, and how to book a time.
