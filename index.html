Utah

Utah (also known as the Beehive State) is a state located in the western United States. It is known for it's gorgeous landscapes, famous national parks, and vast mountain ranges. While southern Utah is known for it's deserts, canyons and arches, the northern half of the state is home to incredible ski resorts with "The Greatest Snow on Earth" according to many skiiers. If you love the outdoors, Utah is the place for you.
